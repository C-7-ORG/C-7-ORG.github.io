About

C-7 is a strategic consulting firm and research group specializing in biosecurity and emerging biotech ventures with an extensive network of key opinion leaders throughout EU, US, Southeast Asia, Latin America, and the Middle East. C-7 provides access to emerging market opportunities, strategic advisory services, and capital formation solutions for cutting-edge research and development projects.

  • Market Intelligence: We maintain comprehensive technical and market coverage across the biotech landscape, providing our partners with asymmetric information advantages and bespoke advisory services throughout a venture's lifecycle.
  • Strategic Partnerships: We cultivate relationships with key decision-makers across institutional investors, research laboratories, manufacturing facilities, and regulatory bodies.

Our network spans leading research institutions, alternative asset managers, regulatory bodies, defense sectors, and critical technology domains. We bring decades of experience navigating the intersection of emerging technologies, risk management, and commercial opportunities.
